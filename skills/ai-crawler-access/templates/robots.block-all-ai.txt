# robots.block-all-ai.txt
# Intura Skills -- ai-crawler-access
# Goal: keep TRADITIONAL search engines (Google, Bing, Apple) but block AI
# crawlers, AI search indexes, AI live-fetch agents, and AI training.
#
# Reminder: robots.txt is VOLUNTARY -- well-behaved bots comply, some (e.g.
# Bytespider) have ignored it. For enforceable blocks, also filter by
# user-agent and the vendors' published IP ranges at your CDN / WAF / firewall.
#
# Merge into your existing robots.txt; keep your private Disallow rules.
# Replace example.com with your domain.

User-agent: Google-Extended
